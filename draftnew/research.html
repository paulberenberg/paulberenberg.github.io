Research

My work focuses on macroeconomics, financial markets, economic policy, regulation, and geoeconomics.

Research Areas

Macroeconomics

Business cycles, inflation, growth, monetary policy and economic forecasting.

Financial Markets

Interest rates, financial stability, capital markets and institutional investors.

Financial Regulation

Regulatory cycles, supervision, prudential policy and market behaviour.

Geoeconomics

Trade policy, strategic competition, economic fragmentation and geopolitical risk.

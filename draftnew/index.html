About

I am a macroeconomist and policy expert working at the intersection of economics, financial markets, public policy and geopolitics.

Since 2022, I have led the Economics and Financial Markets department of the German Insurance Association (GDV), where I advise senior decision-makers on monetary policy, financial stability, regulation and global economic developments.

Before joining GDV, I worked as a researcher at the German Institute for Economic Research (DIW Berlin), contributed to the Joint Economic Forecast of Germany, and conducted research at the Conseil d’Analyse Économique in Paris.

I hold a PhD in Economics from the Hertie School and degrees from Sciences Po, Hertie School and Université Paris II Panthéon-Assas.

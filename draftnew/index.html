About

I advise senior executives, policymakers and financial-market participants on macroeconomic developments, monetary policy, regulation, and geopolitical risks. My professional background combines academic research, policy work, executive education and industry leadership.

Before joining the German Insurance Association, I worked at DIW Berlin and the French Council of Economic Analysis (Conseil d’Analyse Économique). I studied economics and public policy in Berlin and Paris.
